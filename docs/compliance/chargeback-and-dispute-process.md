# Chargeback & Dispute Resolution Process — Dates (dates.care)

Effective: July 2026

## Prevention
1. **Recognizable descriptor**: statements read as DATES.CARE.
2. **Instant confirmation**: purchase receipts are sent immediately upon
   payment confirmation, itemizing the product, amount, and support contact.
3. **Easy cancellation**: subscriptions can be canceled in-app at any time
   before the next billing period; reminders are sent 3 days and 24 hours
   before renewal charges (per our published Payment & Refund Policy).
4. **Clear refund policy**: published in-app (Menu → Refund Policy) and at
   /#payment-refund.

## When a dispute is received
- **Day 0–1**: dispute logged; the account's full transaction ledger,
  login history, message/feature-usage records, and verification status
  are pulled.
- **Day 1–3**: outreach to the customer where possible; refund offered when
  the complaint is legitimate (goodwill resolution beats a chargeback).
- **Within 7 days**: formal response submitted to the processor with
  evidence: itemized ledger entries (timestamps, amounts, balance-after),
  proof of service delivery (feature usage tied to the purchased credits
  or subscription), the customer's 18+ affirmation and verification
  records, and the refund/cancellation policy the customer accepted.

## Monitoring
- Chargeback ratio reviewed monthly with a target below 0.5% and a hard
  internal alarm at 0.8%.
- Every chargeback is root-caused (product confusion, billing descriptor,
  unauthorized use, service complaint) and the pattern addressed.
